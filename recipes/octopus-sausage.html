Ingredients

  • 10 thumb size sausages (or chop 4-5 longer ones into thumb length)
  • drizzle of oil
  • garlic (optional)
  • 2 tbsp siracha hot sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey

Equipments

  • a frying pan
  • a turner spatula

‚û§ Yields up to 5 servings


-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Steps

  1. Line the sausages sideways
  2. Cut the bottom end of the sausages by inserting the knife in the middle and slice all the way through.
    So it's connected on top but splitted on the ends.
  3. Then, rotate the sausages 90 degrees and repeat. Now it's got 4 legs!
  4. I usually fry them at this point to save time but if you want 8 legs simply rotate the sausage,
    hold the legs together to avoid the slits opening up and slice through

    5. - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

  5. Heat some oil and chopped garlic (optional) in a pan, then throw in the sausages and let it cook till the legs flare out.
  6. Once the sausages turn slightly brown, add in siracha and honey.
  7. Let the sauce cook till it's thick and sticky, and it's ready to serve!
